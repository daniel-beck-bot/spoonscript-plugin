Arguments for installation in a non-interactive mode. They will be passed to an installation script generated based on a template:
& install.exe {installer args} | Out-Null

Most common installer args are:
  • /S
  • /S /D=<install directory>
  • /passive /qn
  • /SILENT
  • /VERYSILENT /SUPPRESSMSGBOXES /NORESTART
  • -q
  • -ms
  • --install
